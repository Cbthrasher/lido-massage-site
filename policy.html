Health information

Customers are responsible to inform us of their health condition and well-being before any services are rendered.

Service window

Lido Massage is not accountable for services rendered after 72 hours/3 days. NO EXCEPTION.

Post-massage soreness

It is common to feel some soreness after massage, especially when muscles are tight. It normally goes away after a couple of days.

Gift certificates

All gift certificates from Lido Massage expire after six months and have no cash value. We are not responsible if you cannot redeem the gift during the allotted time.

Belongings

Please watch your own belongings. We are not responsible for them.

Payments

Cash transactions have no fee. A $3 convenience fee is added to all credit/debit card transactions.

Lateness & cancellations

Late arrivals may shorten session time. Please call if you need to reschedule.

Minors

Under 18 must be accompanied by a parent/guardian and provide consent.

Thank you for your cooperation in making your spa experience pleasurable.
