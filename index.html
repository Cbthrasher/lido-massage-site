Lido Massage Policy

Lido Massage reserves the right to refuse service to any customer.

Draping required. Clients are covered during the session; only the area being worked on is exposed.

Payment & gratuity. Payment due at time of service. Tips are appreciated for great service.

All services are for relaxation/wellness and NON-SEXUAL.

Our staff will stop their services immediately when a customer behaves inappropriately. The customer will be responsible to pay for the entire session.

Customers are responsible to inform us of their health condition and well being before any services rendered.

Lido Massage is not accountable for services rendered after 72 hours/3 days. NO EXCEPTION.

It is common to feel some pain after massage especially when your muscles are tight. It will normally go away after a couple of days.

All Gift Certificates from Lido Massage expire after six months. Please note that we are not responsible if you cannot redeem the gift during the allotted time. Our certificates have no cash value.

Please watch your own belongings. We are not responsible for them.

Cash transactions have no fee, but there is a $3 convenience fee added to all credit/debit card transactions.

Lateness & cancellations: Late arrivals may shorten session time. Please call if you need to reschedule.
Minors: Under 18 must be accompanied by a parent/guardian and provide consent.

Thank you for your cooperation in making your spa experience pleasurable.

Long, flowing strokes with light–medium pressure to relax muscles, increase circulation, and reduce stress.
