Our Approach

We keep things simple: caring therapists, organic oils, fresh linens, quiet rooms, and steady, thoughtful technique. Whether you’re here to unwind or to work on tight, sore areas, we tailor each session to you.

What to Expect

Arrive a few minutes early to share any goals or sensitivities. Draping is always required. Walk-ins welcome; you can also call or text to check availability.

Massage Services

Swedish

Long, flowing strokes with light–medium pressure to relax muscles, improve circulation, and reduce stress.

Deep Tissue

Slower, targeted work for stubborn knots and chronic tightness. Back-walking available from trained therapists.

Shiatsu

Japanese acupressure along energy pathways to balance the body and calm the mind. Performed over oil or light lotion.

Thai-Inspired

Assisted stretching and rhythmic pressure to increase mobility and restore energy flow.

Foot & Reflexology

Focused footwork using pressure points that may relieve tension throughout the body; deeply relaxing.

Couples

Two guests enjoy massages together in the same room. Great for date-nights and special occasions.

Prenatal

Side-lying support for comfort and safety; helps ease back, hip, and leg discomfort. (Second trimester+)

Chair Massage

Upright seated massage that focuses on neck, shoulders, and back. Quick relief without oils.

Organic Oils

We use quality, skin-nourishing oils; add aromatherapy or hot stones on request.
