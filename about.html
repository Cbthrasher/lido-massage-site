Massage Styles We Offer

Swedish

Light to medium pressure with long flowing strokes to promote relaxation and reduce stress.

Deep Tissue

Targets deeper muscle layers to release chronic tension and knots.

Shiatsu

Japanese finger pressure technique balancing energy flow and easing tension.

Thai

Assisted stretching and rhythmic pressure to improve flexibility and restore balance.

Foot / Reflexology

Pressure-point massage for the feet, promoting relaxation throughout the body.

Combo Sessions

Popular mixes like Body + Foot or extended sessions for complete relaxation.

Couples Massage

Enjoy a side-by-side massage with a partner, friend, or family member in a shared room.

Prenatal Massage

Gentle, supportive massage tailored for expectant mothers (with doctor’s approval).
