About Lido Massage

We’re a friendly, professional studio in Gainesville, GA. Our licensed therapists use quality organic oils and tailor each session to your needs—from gentle relaxation to targeted deep tissue work.

Swedish

Long, flowing strokes to relax the body, reduce stress, and improve circulation. Great first-timer or recovery massage.

Deep Tissue

Slow, targeted work to release knots and chronic tension. Option for therapist-assisted back-walking where appropriate.

Shiatsu / Chair

Pressure-point technique that balances energy and eases stiffness. Chair massage available for quick relief to neck/shoulders.

Foot Reflexology

Focused foot work that promotes relaxation for the whole body. Available as stand-alone or part of a combo.

Couples

Unwind together in the same room. Perfect for anniversaries, special occasions, or just quality time.

Prenatal

Side-lying, supportive massage designed for comfort during pregnancy. Helps reduce swelling, back pain, and stress.

Add-ons & Notes

  • Organic oils used in every session.
  • Hot stones available upon request (subject to therapist & availability).
  • Please arrive a few minutes early to relax and discuss your goals.
